# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 7 December 2025_

ReUseIt is committed to protecting your privacy and complying with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

## What Data We Collect
- Name, email, phone number (if provided)
- Profile information, posts, comments, and activity
- Device and usage data (for analytics and security)

## How We Use Your Data
- To provide and improve our services
- To personalize your experience
- For security, fraud prevention, and legal compliance

## Your Rights
- **Access:** You can request a copy of your data
- **Correction:** You can update your information
- **Deletion:** You can delete your account and all associated data
- **Export:** You can export your data at any time
- **Withdraw Consent:** You can withdraw consent for data processing

## Data Retention
We retain your data only as long as necessary for the purposes described above or as required by law.

## Data Security
We use encryption and secure storage for your data. Sensitive data is never shared with third parties without your consent.

## Contact
For privacy questions or requests, contact: privacy@reuseit.com

See the app's Settings/Profile section to manage your privacy preferences or delete/export your data.
